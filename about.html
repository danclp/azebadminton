Welcome to AZE Badminton Academy, where our commitment is to deliver an exceptional coaching experience tailored for passionate players at all levels. Our goal is to nurture talent and enhance the skills of both newcomers and seasoned players alike.

About Us

Each of our classes is thoughtfully designed to cater to diverse skill levels, ensuring that every participant enjoys a fun and engaging atmosphere. We focus on a dynamic blend of enjoyment, effective drills, and targeted exercises that elevate every player's game.

Our academy is proudly co-founded and led by head coaches Jerry Ng and Amanda Khoo, both of whom are former Malaysian state players with a wealth of accomplishments. Their expertise and passion for the sport drive the academy’s mission.

Supporting them are our co-founders, Benson Chow and Yvonne Low. Benson, our operations director, is dedicated to exploring innovative business avenues and scouting new talents. Meanwhile, Yvonne spearheads our vibrant social media strategy, connecting with our community and enhancing our online presence.

At AZE Badminton Academy, our vision is to cultivate a passion for badminton within every player, empowering them to reach their highest potential. The name AZE stands for "ACE," where the "Z" symbolizes our unwavering zeal for excellence. We strive to create an environment where enthusiasm and commitment are key, ensuring that each athlete develops not just skills, but also a love for the game. Our goal is to be recognized as the premier badminton academy, producing champions both on and off the court-players who embody excellence, resilience, and sportsmanship.

SME 500 Singapore 2023

The Singapore SME 500 Award was launched to recognise Singapore’s leading Small-Medium Enterprises (SMEs) across major industries and trade sectors.

The award continues to recognises SMEs that have been developed and managed effectively, performed well in its fiscal years, instilled and maintained business excellence in its operations.

Apart from business excellence, the Singapore SME 500 Award honours leading businesses that have proven its success within relating industries, boasting abilities and capabilities to expand and internationalise.

As a recipient of the Singapore SME 500 Award, it does not only affirms one’s strength and qualities in business excellence, but open up doors to local and global opportunities and business network, to continue striving and soar towards greater heights.
