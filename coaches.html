Our Elite
Coaches

At AZE Badminton Academy, our coaching team is the backbone of our success. Each coach brings a wealth of experience and a unique approach to teaching, ensuring that every student receives top-notch guidance. Our dedicated professionals are not only skilled players but also passionate mentors who tailor their coaching methods to suit individual needs, helping students develop their techniques, strategies, and overall game performance.

Beyond technical training, our coaches foster a supportive and motivating environment where players can grow both physically and mentally. They emphasize discipline, perseverance, and sportsmanship, instilling values that extend beyond the court. Whether training beginners or advanced players, our coaching staff is committed to unlocking each athlete’s full potential, making AZE Badminton Academy a place where champions are built.

Head Coach

Jerry Ng

Coach's background

Jerry Ng is a highly respected coach and former Kedah State Player, bringing over 15 years of coaching experience to AZE Badminton Academy. He has served as the Head Coach at Victoria School, where he played a pivotal role in developing young talents. As a BWF Certified Coach, Jerry upholds the highest training standards recognized internationally.

Jerry’s coaching philosophy focuses on creating a positive and engaging environment where players can thrive. He emphasizes skill development and strategic play, ensuring that each student, whether a beginner or advanced, receives personalized attention to reach their full potential.

With his extensive experience, Jerry is dedicated to nurturing talent and instilling a love for the game, guiding his students to achieve their goals on and off the court.

Coach's background

Ang Chia Yu is a dedicated and accomplished coach at AZE Badminton Academy, bringing over 10 years of coaching experience to our team. His extensive experience as a player informs his coaching style, allowing him to effectively mentor and develop our students.

Chia Yu is passionate about fostering a love for the game while focusing on skill development, teamwork, and strategic play, ensuring each player reaches their highest potential.

Head Coach

Goh Jia Le

Coach's background

Goh Jia Le is the Head Coach at AZE Badminton Academy in Johor Bahru, bringing a wealth of experience as a former Johor Bahru State Player. His competitive journey showcases numerous achievements, including a commendable performance at the Majlis Sukan Sekolah Malaysia in 2021.

In 2017, Jia Le was the 1st Runner-Up in the National Junior Circuit B and excelled at the 100 Plus National Junior Circuit Grand Prix Final. His international success includes being the Boys Doubles Champion at the International Junior Championship in 2019.

Jia Le has also made his mark in various other tournaments, clinching the championship title at the Papago Doubles Open in 2022, and securing 2nd Runner-Up in the Junior Purple League 2021 and the Mixed Doubles Open at Papago in 2023.

As a coach, Jia Le is committed to fostering talent and enhancing players' skills through effective training methods and a supportive environment, ensuring that each student reaches their full potential.
