Why us, AZE?

AZE Badminton Academy offers top-tier coaching for players of all levels, focusing on skill development through engaging and effective training. Experienced coaches lead dynamic sessions, while dedicated team members manage operations and community engagement, ensuring a comprehensive and enjoyable learning experience for all participants.

find out more

Low Students Ration We have a maximum of 6-8 students per class to ensure each student gets attended to.

Trained Coaches Our fleet of Coaches are trained by our head coach who led the east zone champion team.

Levels Based Students are grouped together by their levels so as to maximize the coaching quality.

Confidence We are confident to help our students improve efficiently.

Programmes

Kids Novice

  • Basic footworks
  • Proper Gripping Techniques
  • Basic Strokes (Service, Clear, Net, Tap, Drop)
  • Basic Defence
  • Agility
  • Speed

Kids/Competitive Class/Adult Intermediate

  • Four side corner footwork
  • V Shape Footwork
  • Full court footwork
  • Intermediate strokes (Net Kill, Cross Net, Jump Smash, Backhand etc)
  • Singles drills
  • Doubles rotation & coverage
  • Physical Training
  • Agility
