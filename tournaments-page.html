Tournament Rules & Regulations

  1. Players who choose to join in multiple categories will not be given time to rest should there be consecutive matches.
  2. Maximum 3 categories per player
  3. No replacement/substitution once the registration is closed no matter what the reason is.
  4. No refunds will be given no matter what the reason is. (MC included)
  5. Players must bring along their ID for registration & for verification by the umpire before the start of each match.
  6. All players must stay behind for line judge duties after their match, failure to do so will result in instant disqualifications.
  7. All winners must stay until the end of the tournament for prize giving ceremony.
  8. It is the player’s responsibility to check and ensure eligibility, failure to do so will result in walkover.
  9. It is the player’s responsibility to ensure the names/categories are correct before the deadline given.
  10. Organizer reserves the right to reduce prize/prize money due to insufficient entries per category.
  11. This tournament is conducted by Tournament Software.
  12. The organizer reserves all rights to amend the rules & regulations.
